Practical, Bible-based messages rooted in the Word of God. Relatable, encouraging, and designed to speak to real life.

Family-oriented, warm, and Spirit-led. We're a multigenerational community where youth, young adults, families, and seniors all feel at home. Come as you are — casual or dressed up, you're welcome.

Your Kids Are in Good Hands

We have a full Kids Ministry running during Sunday service so your children can learn about Jesus in a fun, age-appropriate, and safe environment. Kids are checked in before service and will have their own worship, teaching, and activities tailored just for them.
